Conclusion

Hopefully, we can all agree now that interest rates are an extremely powerful tool.

However, many people, especially the poor often ignore the consequences of high interest rates.

This is due to the psychological actions of humans overvaluing the importance of current gradification as opposed to waiting a few days.

Think about this if you stand close to a small house, that house is all you can see and you might be tempted to live there. However if you take a few steps back you might be ablt to see a skyscrapper of wealth behing it. This reveals the importance of delaying gratification and earning much more over the long run.
