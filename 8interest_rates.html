Conclusion

Interest Rates are obviously extremely powerful tool.

However, many people, especially the poor, often ignore the consequences of high interest rates.

This is due to the psychological actions of humans overvaluing the importance of current gradification as opposed to waiting a few days.

Think, if you stand close to a nice small house, that house is all you can see. However, there could be a skyscrapper of wealth behing it. All you need to do is take a few steps backwards and learn that by delaying gratification you can earn much more over the long run.
