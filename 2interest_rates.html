Time Value of Money

Let me Start by asking you a simple question.

What would you prefer?

******BUTTON $20 Today | $25 in Two Weeks

Let's get even easier. What would you prefer?

*******BUTTON A dollar today | A dollar tommorrow?

Congratulations! This concept defintes the basics of personal finance.

Definition: Time Value of Money is the idea that a dollar today is worth more than a dollar in the future, because the dollar received today can earn INTEREST up until the time the future dollar is received.
